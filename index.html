About Dr. Saba Asad

Science, compassion, and genuine care

Dr. Saba Asad is a qualified psychiatrist with over a decade of clinical experience helping individuals overcome mental health challenges. She combines the latest evidence-based treatments with a deeply human approach — creating a safe space where patients feel truly heard.

Having trained at leading medical institutions in Pakistan, Dr. Asad specializes in anxiety, depression, OCD, PTSD, bipolar disorder, and more. She sees patients in Lahore, Gujranwala and conducts online consultations nationwide.

How It Works

Your journey to wellbeing, step by step

1

Initial Consultation

A comprehensive 60-minute assessment to understand your history, symptoms, and goals — in a safe, confidential setting.

2

Personalised Plan

Dr. Asad creates a tailored treatment plan — which may include therapy, medication, or both — specific to your needs.

3

Regular Sessions

Weekly or fortnightly sessions to work through your plan, track progress, and adjust treatment as you grow.

4

Lasting Recovery

Build long-term tools, resilience, and strategies so you can thrive — with ongoing support available whenever needed.
